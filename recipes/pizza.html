Pizza Recipe

Description:

A traditional, easy and delicious pizza recipe for you to make at home, and enjoy by yourself or with your friends.

dough

  • 330g tipo00 flour
  • 220ml lukewarm water
  • 9g salt
  • 7g yeast

sauce

  • 1 can canned tomatos
  • 1 pinch dryed basil
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 pinch dryed garlic

cheese! and toppings

  • 1 ball fior di latte mozz
  • chili infused honey

Steps

  1. make the dough

    add all ingredients together and let rest

  2. preheat oven to a ton of degrees
  3. make the sauce
  4. add dough to a pan and let it rest
  5. add sauce onto the rested dough
  6. add cheese!
  7. bake at a gadzillion degrees for aprox 2 mins
  8. drizzle honey ontop of your pizza
  9. let it cool for a minute and enjoy
