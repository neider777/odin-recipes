Pulled Chicken Recipe

Description

A quick and easy Pulled Chicken recipe, for you to enjoy for yourself or with your friends.

Very low calorie and perfect for meal prep.

meat

  • 2kg Chicken breast
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 1l of water or chicken stock
  • 20g salt

flavors

  • 20g garlic powder
  • 20g onion powder
  • 15g chipotle powder
  • 10g salt
  • 5g msg
